Laning Guide

In MLBB we have 5 role such as


EXPLANE


MIDLANE


GOLDLANE


JUNGLER


ROAMER

1.EXPLANE

three things you must do in EXPLANE

  1. Watch for turtle/lord and help the jungler zone the enemy so the enemy dont come close to the lord or turtle
  2. if your hero power win over the enemy then pressure the enemy as much as you can
  3. fit on your hero role, there's 2 explane type, sustain and damage
    • sustain explaner usually tank their teammate and usually set up/open the war
    • damage explaner usually play as the third damage dealer of the team. this type of explaner usually do some split push and some pick of on the enemy mage or marksman

this is a full guide for explane

2.MIDLANE

three things you must do in MIDLANE

  1. Help your jungler kill the buff in the early game by hitting the buff with a skill once then continue to clear the waves
  2. always clear the minion and dont let the turret be destroyed because midlane turret is the most important outer turret among them all
  3. after clearing the minion wave always rotate either to explane, goldlane or help your jungler pick of some of the enemy heroes

this video contains full guide for midlane

3.GOLDLANE

Three things you must do in GOLDLANE

  1. Try not to die while in laning phase
  2. prioritize on farming because you're the damage dealer of the team in the late game
  3. mind the distance between you and enemy so you're not easy to get killed

this video contains full guide for goldlane

4.Jungler

Three things you must do as a Jungler

  1. Try not to lose retribution contest while you are in a war at the turtle or lord
  2. kill enemy hero as much as you can so you can get into snowball phase(a phase where you kill much enemy hero without dying)
  3. prioritize farming the jungle because you are the second damage dealer in the team

this video contains full guide for jungler

5.Roamer

Three things you must do as a roamer

  1. roam and help your teammate as much as you can
  2. open the map/bushes as much as you can
  3. understand your roamer type. there's two types of roamer
    • tanker has the same role as the sustain explaner, but they dont focus on laning, they focus on tanking the damage for the team
    • healer usually heals the team and sticking into the damage dealer so they dont die

this video contains full guide for roamer
